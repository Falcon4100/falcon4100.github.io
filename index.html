The movement of The Brotherhood

The Brotherhood clan was started to bring a group of friends together who liked playing fortnite. The Brotherhood was founded on May 10, 2018.

What do we do?

We currently don't compete, as we aren't big enough, but we do play a lot of fortnite, waiting for the day that we enter a competiton. We are always looking for new, skilled members to join our team, as well.

Want to Join?

If you want to join, and you think that you have what it takes, make sure that you fit all of these categories:

  • You play on PS4
  • You have a lot of free time
  • You are flexible
  • You live in the United States
  • You are good at Fortnite

If you fit all of those, then shoot us an email at example@noreply.com
